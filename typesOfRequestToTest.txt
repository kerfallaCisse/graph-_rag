Exploratory query :
This type of query focuses on exploring the dataset to uncover hidden patterns, connections, or properties about wines, grapes, or wineries.

Query : What are the key characteristics of wines produced by the winery "In vino veritas" in different countries?

Connecting the dots :
This query requires linking different pieces of information to generate an answer that spans multiple entities or classes in the knowledge graph.

Query : Which grape varieties are used to make dry wines, and what are the typical food pairings for these wines?
